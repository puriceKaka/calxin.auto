Our Story

Founded in 2020, Calxin Auto Mombasa has been at the forefront of the automotive industry, providing quality vehicles and exceptional service to customers across Mombasa and beyond. What started as a small dealership has grown into one of the region's most trusted auto retailers.

Our commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and integrity has made us the go-to destination for premium vehicles, spare parts, and automotive solutions.
