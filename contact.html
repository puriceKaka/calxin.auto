Frequently Asked Questions

What are your payment options?

We accept cash, bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa), and installment plans. Our team will help you choose the best option for your purchase.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We partner with leading financial institutions to offer flexible financing options with competitive interest rates and flexible payment terms.

What warranty do you provide?

All our vehicles come with a comprehensive warranty covering major mechanical issues for up to 2 years or based on mileage, whichever comes first.

Can I test drive a vehicle?

Absolutely! We encourage all customers to test drive any vehicle before purchasing. Just visit our showroom or call us to schedule a test drive.

Do you accept trade-ins?

Yes, we accept trade-ins! We'll evaluate your current vehicle and provide a competitive offer to apply towards your new purchase.
